About Me

A committed life-long learner I truly enjoy all things technical. Starting with the first VCR I took apart after finding a screwdriver, to the computer I built when I decided it was time to upgrade. I truly enjoy taking things apart, building new things, as well as innovating ideas. I just love to tinker, my past life as a mechanic stays with me. On top of Programming I have a 5 bicycles I love rebuild. I have completely stripped each one of my bicycles and serviced them up from top to bottom.

A challenge I also enjoy is working on My E39 BMW. My technical skills and automotive ability is something I’m very proud of and is something I give much credit to my father. Always wanting a BMW himself but unable to due to his children, my father lived below his means always admiring these “Ultimate Driving Machines” from a distance. I’m very proud to say we bought this car together with the confidence that I would be able to fix just about anything that goes wrong. On initial purchase I spent around $1500 on parts to save myself about $3000 worth of labor at the dealership.
